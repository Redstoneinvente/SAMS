Teacher Guide

Welcome to the Teacher Guide section of the application! This guide will walk you through the steps to effectively use the features available for managing your class and accessing important information.

Logging In (First Time Only)

  1. Open the application and click on the "Login" button.
  2. Enter your provided credentials (username and password) to access your account.

Homepage Navigation

On the homepage, you'll find a range of features designed to help you manage your class efficiently.

Attendance Management

  1. Click on the "Manage Class" option under "Current Class."
  2. Here, you'll see a list of students in your class along with their attendance status.
  3. To mark a student as present or absent, simply check or uncheck the corresponding box next to their name.
  4. To load the full class list, click on the eye icon.
  5. Utilize the search bar to quickly find specific students by entering their names.
  6. If you need to generate attendance reports, select a student to view their individual attendance history.

Timetable Viewing

  1. On the homepage, locate the "View Timetable" option.
  2. Click on it to access your weekly timetable.
  3. Here, you'll be able to view your schedule for each day of the week, including class timings and subjects.

Tips for Effective Use

  • Keep your attendance records up to date to ensure accurate reporting.
  • Utilize the search bar to quickly find specific students within your class.
  • Regularly check your weekly timetable to stay organized and prepared for your classes.
Remember, this guide provides an overview of the key features available in the Teacher Guide section. If you have any questions or encounter any issues while using the application, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.
Happy teaching and managing your class with our application!
